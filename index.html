3
Regulated Enterprise

Private Offer Architecture for FSI + HCLS Buyers

A public Marketplace listing is necessary but not sufficient for regulated buyers. FSI and HCLS accounts require custom pricing, MSA terms, compliance documentation (SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA BAA, FedRAMP path), and EDP drawdown eligibility — all of which CPPO private offers deliver without a direct enterprise sales cycle.

Design the private offer framework for Mistral's top-10 US regulated enterprise targets. The procurement friction disappears; the deal moves at the speed of the partner relationship. This is the unlock for the exact customer segment where Mistral's sovereign AI story lands hardest.
